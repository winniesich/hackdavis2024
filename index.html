What we provide

Shelter support services

Offering emergency, transitional, family, and permanent supportive housing from three different locations:

  • Paul's Place
  • Cesar Chavez Plaza
  • Creekside

Meal support services

Providing meals to seniors, low-income, and homeless. Ingredients are sourced from local markets and donated by individuals, groups, businesses, non-profits, and government agencies
